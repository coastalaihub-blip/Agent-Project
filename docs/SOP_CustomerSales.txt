================================================================================
SECTION 1 - THE CUSTOMER'S WORLD (THE PROBLEM)
================================================================================

WHO THE CUSTOMER IS
A clinic owner or small business owner in India. They see patients, manage staff,
handle billing, and run operations -- often all at once. A dedicated front-desk
receptionist is either too expensive, unreliable, or simply unavailable after hours.

THEIR DAILY REALITY
- Phone rings while they're with a patient -- no one picks up
- A patient calls at 8pm to book an appointment -- the clinic is closed
- The receptionist is on leave -- calls go unanswered all day
- They rely on WhatsApp to book appointments, but messages pile up

THE PAIN IN NUMBERS
- Average missed calls per day: 5
- Average appointment value: Rs. 3,000
- Revenue lost per day: Rs. 15,000
- Revenue lost per month: Rs. 4,50,000

THE EMOTIONAL PAIN
- Guilt of missing a patient who needed help
- Stress of being the only person who can handle everything
- Fear of losing patients to a nearby clinic that always picks up
- Exhaustion of managing calls on top of clinical work

================================================================================
SECTION 2 - WHAT THEY GET (PRODUCT FROM CUSTOMER POV)
================================================================================

The customer does not need to understand AI, APIs, or servers.
Here is what they experience:

- A phone number that always answers -- 24/7, no holidays, no sick days, no salary
- The AI speaks in their clinic's name: "Thank you for calling Bright Smile Dental
  Clinic. How can I help you today?"
- Books appointments automatically -- caller gives name and time; confirmed instantly,
  no staff involvement
- Sends an SMS confirmation to the caller immediately after booking
- Owner opens the app to see every call, every booking, every transcript -- from
  anywhere, at any time
- Escalation push notification -- if a caller is upset or needs a human, the owner's
  phone buzzes immediately
- Setup in 3 minutes -- answer 5 questions in the app. Done.

SECTION 3 - THE SALES JOURNEY (END-TO-END PROCESS)
================================================================================

STAGE 1 -- PROSPECT

Where to find customers:
- Local WhatsApp doctor/clinic groups
- Google Maps -- search "clinic near [area]", look for no website or reviews
  mentioning missed calls
- Doctor directories (Practo, Justdial listings)
- Personal and alumni networks

Trigger signals -- prioritize clinics that:
- Have no visible receptionist
- Have Google reviews mentioning "no one picks up"
- Are a solo-doctor practice
- Are open evenings or weekends with no obvious staff coverage

Outreach channel: WhatsApp first. Call only if no response after 48 hours.

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 2 -- FIRST TOUCH (THE HOOK)

Goal: Get them to agree to a 2-minute demo (live call or video screen share).

Lead with the pain -- not the technology.

Do NOT say: "We've built an AI-powered LLM voice agent with RAG-based FAQ retrieval."

SAY: "Most clinics miss 5 calls a day. At Rs. 3,000 per appointment, that's
Rs. 15,000 gone every single day. We fix that."

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 3 -- DEMO

- Run the 3-step live demo from demo_script.md:
  1. Sign up in the app (5 questions)
  2. Call the AI number -- watch it answer and book
  3. Open dashboard -- see the transcript and booking
- Keep the demo to 90 seconds. Let the product speak.
- Always have a backup screen recording ready in case of technical issues.

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 4 -- HANDLE OBJECTIONS

"What if it says the wrong thing?"
  -> It only answers from information you've approved. If it doesn't know,
     it escalates to you immediately.

"What about Hindi/regional languages?"
  -> English is live now. Hindi is coming in the next phase -- as a pilot
     customer, you'll get it free.

"Is it expensive?"
  -> Free for 3 months for the first 50 businesses. Rs. 999/month after that.

"How long to set up?"
  -> 3 minutes -- you just watched it.

"What if I already have a receptionist?"
  -> The AI handles overflow and after-hours calls. Your receptionist keeps
     their job.

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 5 -- CLOSE

Ask directly:
"Would you like to be one of our first 50 free pilot businesses?"

If yes -- collect:
- Full name
- Clinic name
- Phone number
- Preferred language (English / Hindi -- note Hindi is upcoming)

Next step:
- Send them a TestFlight invite (iOS) or APK link (Android)

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 6 -- ONBOARD

Walk them through the 5 onboarding questions in the app:

1. What type of business?           -> Clinic
2. What are your hours?             -> e.g., 9am-6pm, Monday-Saturday
3. What do callers usually want?    -> Appointments / Information / Callback
4. What should the AI do if it
   can't help?                      -> Escalate to owner / Take a message
5. Do you want appointment
   booking enabled?                 -> Yes / No

Their number goes live immediately after the 5th answer. No tech setup required.

------------------------------------------------------------

STAGE 7 -- FOLLOW-UP (DAY 3 & DAY 7)

Day 3 check-in:
"Hi [Name], just checking in -- how many calls has the AI handled so far?
Any questions?"

Day 7 review:
- Share call log stats from the dashboard
- Show them the value in numbers (calls handled, appointments booked, escalations)
- Ask for a referral: "Do you know another clinic owner who'd find this useful?"

SECTION 7 - OBJECTION CHEAT SHEET
================================================================================

"I already have a receptionist"
  -> The AI handles overflow and after-hours -- your receptionist stays, and
     nothing falls through the cracks.

"What if it makes mistakes?"
  -> It only answers what you've approved. Unknown questions are escalated to
     you -- it never guesses.

"I'm not a tech person"
  -> 3 minutes. 5 questions. No tech knowledge needed -- you just saw it work live.

"Is my data safe?"
  -> All data is stored on secure servers. Calls and transcripts are private
     to your account only.

"Hindi/regional language?"
  -> English is live. Hindi is coming in the next phase -- pilot customers get it free.

"What if the AI is busy?"
  -> It handles unlimited simultaneous calls. No busy signal. Ever.

"What if internet goes down?"
  -> The AI runs on cloud infrastructure -- your clinic's internet doesn't affect it.

"Can I cancel anytime?"
  -> Yes -- month to month after the free period. No lock-in.
