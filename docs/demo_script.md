# Demo Script — March 28 Summit

## The 90-Second Pitch

> "You're a dentist. You're inside with a patient.
> Your phone rings. You miss it.
> That caller wanted to book a ₹3,000 appointment.
> That happens 5 times a day. You've lost ₹15,000 — and you don't even know it.
>
> [pause — let it land]
>
> What if you never missed another call?"

---

## Live Demo — Step by Step

### Step 1: Sign up as a new clinic (30 seconds)
- Open app on iPhone (from TestFlight)
- Tap "Get Started"
- Answer 5 questions: Clinic → 9am–6pm → Appointments → Callback → Booking
- Screen shows: "Your number is +91 80 4500 1001. You're live."

**Say:** "That took under 3 minutes. The AI is now live on this number."

---

### Step 2: Call the demo number (45 seconds)

- [Call +91 80 4500 1001 from another phone — audience can hear it on speaker]

**Call script:**
1. AI answers: "Thank you for calling Bright Smile Dental Clinic. How can I help you today?"
2. Say: "I'd like to book an appointment for Thursday at 11am."
3. AI: "Thursday at 11am — I'll book that for you. Can I have your name please?"
4. Say: "Priya Nair"
5. AI: "Thank you Priya. Your appointment is confirmed for Thursday at 11am. You'll receive an SMS shortly."

**Say:** "That call is now in our system."

---

### Step 3: Show the dashboard (15 seconds)
- Open app → Dashboard
- Show: 1 call logged, 1 appointment booked
- Tap the call → show transcript + summary

**Say:** "The dentist finishes with their patient. Opens the app. Sees the appointment. Didn't lift a finger."

---

### Step 4 (optional if time): Escalation

- Call again. Say: "I need to speak to someone urgently."
- AI: "Of course, let me connect you right away."
- Founder's phone gets a push notification: "Caller on line — urgent"

**Say:** "The AI knows when to step aside."

---

### Step 5: Call Center demo (if time allows)

- Call second demo number
- Ask a FAQ: "What are your business hours?"
- AI answers correctly
- Ask to escalate → AI escalates

---

## Handling Questions

**"What if the AI says the wrong thing?"**
> "It only answers from verified information. If it doesn't know something, it says so and connects to staff. It never guesses."

**"What languages does it support?"**
> "English right now — Hindi and regional languages in the next phase."

**"How much does it cost?"**
> "We're in beta. First 50 businesses get free access for 3 months. After that, ₹999/month."

**"What's the setup time?"**
> "Under 3 minutes — you just saw it."

---

## What NOT to do

- Don't demo if voice sounds robotic — describe it instead
- Don't let the call go to more than 3 turns — keep it crisp
- Don't show settings screen — not polished enough
- If anything fails → "Let me show you the recording from our test call" (have backup video)
